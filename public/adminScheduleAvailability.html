Set Up Your Calendly Event

Step 1: Log in to Calendly or create an account. Click Create > Event Type > One-on-One to set up your event with your preferred availability and settings.

Step 2: From the event type page, choose Share and copy the generated link.

Step 3: Paste your Calendly link in the box above, then click Submit to integrate it with Mindsphere.
